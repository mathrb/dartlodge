# Golf

## Overview
A game inspired by golf, where players aim to complete each "hole" (number) in as few darts as possible. The game is played over 18 "holes," with each hole focusing on a specific number.

## Rules
- **Holes**: 18 holes, focusing on numbers 1 through 18
- **Objective**: Complete each hole in as few darts as possible
- **Darts per Turn**: Unlimited darts per hole
- **Scoring**: The number of darts thrown to hit the number of the hole is recorded. The player with the lowest total number of darts after 18 holes wins
- **Winning**: The player with the lowest total score after 18 holes wins

## Scoring
- **Single**: Counts as a hit
- **Double/Triple**: Also counts as a hit

## Variations
- **Double Golf**: Players must hit doubles to complete a hole
- **Triple Golf**: Players must hit triples to complete a hole

## Implementation Notes
- Track darts thrown per hole
- Golf-themed scoring (lower is better)
- Good for precision practice
