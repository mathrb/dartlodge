# Chase the Dragon

## Overview
A game where players aim to hit specific segments on the dartboard in sequence. The goal is to "chase the dragon" by hitting all the required segments.

## Rules
- **Segments**: Players must hit specific segments in sequence (e.g., 20, 1, 18, 4, 13, 6, 10, 15, 2, 17, 3, 19, 7, 16, 8, 11, 14, 9, 12, 5, 20)
- **Objective**: Hit all segments in sequence
- **Darts per Turn**: 3 darts
- **Winning**: The first player to hit all segments in sequence wins

## Scoring
- **Single**: Counts as a hit
- **Double/Triple**: Also counts as a hit

## Variations
- **Double Chase the Dragon**: Players must hit doubles for each segment
- **Triple Chase the Dragon**: Players must hit triples for each segment

## Implementation Notes
- Predefined sequence of target segments
- Track current position in sequence for each player
- Good for improving accuracy across the board
