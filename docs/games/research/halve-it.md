# Halve-It

## Overview
A game where players aim to hit specific numbers to avoid losing half their score. The game is played over 20 rounds, with each round focusing on a specific number.

## Rules
- **Rounds**: 20 rounds, focusing on numbers 1 through 20
- **Objective**: Hit the number of the round to avoid losing half your score
- **Darts per Turn**: 3 darts
- **Scoring**: Players start with a set score (e.g., 1000). If a player fails to hit the number of the round, their score is halved
- **Winning**: The player with the highest score after 20 rounds wins

## Scoring
- **Single**: Counts as a hit
- **Double/Triple**: Also counts as a hit

## Variations
- **Double Halve-It**: Players must hit doubles to avoid losing half their score
- **Triple Halve-It**: Players must hit triples to avoid losing half their score

## Implementation Notes
- Track current round and target number
- Implement score halving logic
- Good for practicing specific numbers
