# Killer

## Overview
A game where players aim to "kill" their opponents by hitting their assigned number. Each player is assigned a number, and the goal is to hit that number to reduce the opponent's "lives."

## Rules
- **Assignment**: Each player is assigned a number (e.g., 1 through 20)
- **Lives**: Each player starts with a set number of lives (e.g., 3)
- **Objective**: Hit the opponent's number to reduce their lives
- **Winning**: The last player with lives remaining wins

## Scoring
- **Single**: Reduces one life
- **Double**: Reduces two lives
- **Triple**: Reduces three lives

## Variations
- **Team Killer**: Players form teams, and the goal is to eliminate the opposing team
- **Blind Killer**: Players do not know their opponent's number

## Implementation Notes
- Random number assignment at game start
- Track lives for each player
- Multiplayer focused game mode
