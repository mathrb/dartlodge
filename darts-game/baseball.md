# Baseball

## Overview
A game inspired by baseball, where players aim to score "runs" by hitting specific numbers. The game is played over 9 "innings," with each inning focusing on a specific number.

## Rules
- **Innings**: 9 innings, focusing on numbers 1 through 9
- **Objective**: Score as many "runs" as possible by hitting the number of the inning
- **Darts per Turn**: 3 darts
- **Scoring**: Each hit on the number of the inning scores a "run." Doubles and triples count as multiple hits
- **Winning**: The player with the highest number of "runs" after 9 innings wins

## Scoring
- **Single**: 1 run
- **Double**: 2 runs
- **Triple**: 3 runs

## Variations
- **Double Baseball**: Players must hit doubles to score runs
- **Triple Baseball**: Players must hit triples to score runs
