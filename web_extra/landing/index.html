Everything in one app

Built for players who love the detail.

From a quick 501 to a full statistical breakdown of your throwing — DartLodge keeps score and turns it into insight.

🎯

Every game mode

X01, Cricket (standard, cut-throat, no-score · fixed, random & crazy targets), Around the Clock, Bob's 27, Catch 40, Shanghai, Count-Up and Checkout Practice.

📊

Deep statistics

3-dart and first-nine averages, checkout %, highest checkout, cricket marks-per-round, score & mark distributions — updated automatically as you play.

🔥

Impact heatmaps

See exactly where your darts land, per game and across your whole history, on a density heatmap of the board.

📷

Camera auto-scoring Beta

Point your camera at the board and let DartLodge score automatically. Experimental and still learning — runs entirely on your device.

🔒

Private by design

No account, no sign-up, no cloud. Your players, games and stats are stored locally and never sold or shared.

🏆

Achievements & history

Unlock achievements, track multiple players, and browse a full game history with per-leg breakdowns.
